WHOOP Daily Briefings — Setup Guide

Connect your WHOOP and get AI-powered health coaching via WhatsApp, twice a day.

What You'll Get

Two WhatsApp messages a day — at 7:00 AM and 8:00 PM — from a brutally honest Spanish-speaking fitness coach who reads your WHOOP data so you can't lie to yourself. Here's what to expect:

Morning — Good recovery
💤 Sueño: 7h 45min, eficiencia 89% ❤️ Recuperación: 82% (verde) HRV 12% por encima de tu media. 🏃 Esfuerzo ayer: 15.3 (alto) Cuerpo listo. Aprovecha o habrás desperdiciado la mejor forma que tenías en semanas.
Mornings cover sleep, recovery & yesterday's strain
Morning — Bad recovery
💤 Sueño: 5h 20min, eficiencia 71% Deuda acumulada: 4.2h. Sueño profundo bajo. ❤️ Recuperación: 18% (rojo) HRV un 23% bajo tu baseline. 🏃 Esfuerzo ayer: 16.8 (máximo) Entrenaste al límite con el cuerpo al 18%. Enhorabuena por saber escucharte. Hoy: descanso o te rompes.
Insults are data-driven, not random
Evening — Strain & trends
🏃 Esfuerzo hoy: 14.2 (alto) Pádel — 62min, esfuerzo 14.2 📊 Tendencia 7 días: ascendente 4 días por encima de 14 — el cuerpo acumula. Mañana decide si eres inteligente o simplemente terco.
Evenings cover today's workouts and 7-day strain trend

Setup — 6 Steps

1
Sign Up on the Web App

Go to [APP-URL — update me] and enter your email address. You'll receive a magic link — click it to log in. No password needed.

Once logged in, the app will ask for your WHOOP account email to create a beta access request. Fill it in and submit.

2
Wait for Approval

The admin (Luis) will receive a notification and will:

  • Add your WHOOP email as a test user in the WHOOP Developer Portal
  • Approve your beta request in the admin panel

You'll get a confirmation email when you're approved. This is a manual step — expect up to a few hours.

3
Connect Your WHOOP Account

After approval, return to the app and click Connect WHOOP. This starts an OAuth flow — you'll be redirected to WHOOP to authorize access.

The app requests read-only access to: recovery, sleep, cycles, workouts, and profile data. No write permissions.

4
Set Your Preferences

Once WHOOP is connected, configure two things:

  • Bot email address — the email inbox your Claude Code agent monitors (where the WHOOP data emails will be sent)
  • Timezone — Europe/Madrid (mainland Spain) or Atlantic/Canary (Canary Islands)

The app sends two emails daily to your bot email: one at 7:00 AM and one at 8:00 PM, local to your timezone.

5
Install the Interpretation Skill

Download the skill package, unzip it, and move the folder into your agent's skills/ directory:

whoop-interpretation-skill.zip SKILL.md + _meta.json — v1.0.0
Download

After unzipping, move the folder here:

~/.openclaw/ your-agent/ skills/ whoop-interpretation-skill/ ← move here SKILL.md _meta.json
ℹ️ The exact path depends on your OpenClaw setup. Replace your-agent with the actual name of your agent directory.
6
Set Up the Cron Schedule

The skill needs to run at 7:00 AM and 8:00 PM every day. In Claude Code, paste this prompt to create both scheduled tasks at once:

Create two durable scheduled tasks:

1. Every day at 7:03 AM local time — prompt:
"Run the whoop-interpretation-skill: check for the latest
WHOOP morning data email, interpret the health metrics
using the skill, and send the WhatsApp briefing message."

2. Every day at 8:03 PM local time — prompt:
"Run the whoop-interpretation-skill: check for the latest
WHOOP evening data email, interpret the health metrics
using the skill, and send the WhatsApp briefing message."

Make both durable so they persist across restarts.
⚠️ Claude Code must be running at 7:03 AM and 8:03 PM for the cron to fire. Keep it open in a persistent terminal session on a machine that's on at those times.

That's it. The skill handles everything else: finding the right email, parsing the JSON payload, interpreting the health data, and sending the WhatsApp message.

Troubleshooting

I connected WHOOP but no emails are arriving
Check that your timezone is set correctly in app preferences (mainland Spain vs. Canary Islands). Reports fire at exactly 7:00 AM and 8:00 PM local time — they won't arrive early or late. Also confirm your beta approval is showing as approved in the app.
The skill isn't finding the emails
Make sure the bot email address you entered in the app's preferences is the same inbox your Claude Code agent has access to. The system sends to exactly that address — if there's a mismatch, emails go nowhere your agent can read.
The scheduled tasks aren't firing
Durable cron jobs persist across restarts but Claude Code must be running at the scheduled time. If it's closed at 7:03 AM, that run is skipped. There's no catch-up mechanism.
WhatsApp messages are in Spanish
By design. The coach personality is Spanish-language only. Insults do not translate well.
